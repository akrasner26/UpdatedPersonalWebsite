Who I Am

I am currently a Computer Science student at Pace University. These last four years have been quite a journey for me. At the end of Freshman year, I became the president of Pace Computing Society, which is one of the largest tech clubs on campus. That summer, I also organized a team of students to work together to complete projects for outside businesses. Seidenberg Creative Labs (or SCL for short) became my job after that and together with my team, we have created websites, apps, and even a virtual tour of our school for potential students to see. What I Do

I started out as another developer on the team. I was creating wireframes and coding wherever I could. Once we started getting more and more projects, I found myself becoming a Project Manager. At one point, we had 4 projects going on at the same time with about 20 people working on the team. It was my job to split everyone up accordingly and make sure that the communication between the clients and the team were clear. I made sure that deadlines were met and to help me, I started implimenting the Scrum methodology and got a Scrum Master certification.
